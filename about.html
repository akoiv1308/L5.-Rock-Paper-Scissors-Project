Rock Paper Scissors

Sam and Karen's Rock Paper Scissor Game Website

History

Sam Kass and Karen Bryla one day played rock, paper, scissors. However, they realized that the game is repetitive and does not bring much joy. Hence, both decided to create something better and more enjoyable, and they called it "rock, paper, scissors, lizard spock" game. This game became extremely popular among their friends and has spread around the globe in couple of weeks.

Game Rules

Rock, paper scissors, lizard, spock is a version Rock, paper scissors that was created by Sam and Karen. It inherits the same concept from the original game, however, it adds couple of moved, thus, reducing the probability of a tie. The game is played where players deliver hand signals that will represent the elements of the game; rock, paper and scissors. The outcome of the game is determined by 3 simple rules: rock wins against scissor, Scissors win against paper, paper wins against rock. For Sam and Karen's game refer to the diagram on the right.


Biography of Sam and Karen

Even though Karen enjoys playing sport, in her spare time, her hobby involved playing board games. She also attends Chess and Board Game Club and along with Sam, she decided to create an easy and super-interactive game that would allow her classmates to play in between periods.

This website allows users to play rock, paper, scissors, lizard, and spock with each other, tell the history of the game and buy merchandise.
